Here you will find noise generators corresponding to each channel (blue, green, etc.). They generate synthetic noise according to the statistics derived from the corresponding experiments. The basic component of each noise is Gaussian with a specific power spectral density (non-white). In addition, the library contains theoretical noise, which represents the Gaussian component of the ambient noise. The power of the noise within a given bandwidth can be adjusted by the user.

Download noise statistics files [here](), then go to [Github](), download and run `generate_noise.m`.

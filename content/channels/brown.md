* Experiment:
  * Location: from southern California to east of Hawaii
  * Date recorded: November 1994
  * Center frequency: 75 Hz
  * Bandwidth: 37.5 Hz
  * Symbol rate: R = 37.5 Hz
  * Distance: 3250 km
  * Tx/Rx/water depth: 652/900/x
  * Mobility: mobile
  * Receiving array: 20-element vertical array, 35 m spacing, 900 m – 1600 m depth
* Parameters:
  * Sampling rate in delay: 2R
  * Total delay coverage: up to 7 seconds
  * Sampling rate in time: R/10 for the channel response, 2R for the phase/delay
  * Total time coverage: 13 minutes
